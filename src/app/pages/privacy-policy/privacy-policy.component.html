Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

PlainClient is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the application.

Information We Collect
  • Account information such as email address and display name
  • Business data including clients, contacts, tasks, and profits
  • Personal tasks and personal profit entries you create
How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to provide core application functionality, including client management, task tracking, profit tracking, and team collaboration. Personal tasks and personal profits are private to your account and are never visible to team members.

Data Storage & Security

Data is securely stored using industry-standard cloud infrastructure. Reasonable safeguards are in place to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Team Access & Privacy

Team members can only access business-related data that you explicitly share with them. Personal data, including personal profits and tasks, remains private to you at all times.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact the application owner.
